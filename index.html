📤 Sharing Scores Between Groups
How It Works
When two foursomes are playing different courses or tee times, each group's scorer enters their own scores. After the round, you merge them together.
Step 1: You (Group 1 Scorer)
Enter scores for your foursome as you play. When done, tap 📤 Export — this copies your group's scores to clipboard.
Step 2: Send to Other Scorer
Paste the code in a text message or iMessage to the other group's scorer.
Step 3: Other Scorer Imports
They tap 📥 Import, paste the code you sent, and your scores merge into their app. They do the same back to you.
Result
Both phones now have complete scores for all 8 players. Game settlements, scorecards, and standings are fully up to date.
